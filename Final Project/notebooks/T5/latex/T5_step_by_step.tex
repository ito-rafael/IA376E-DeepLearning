\section*{0. Dataset and Description}\label{dataset-and-description}}

\textbf{Name:} CoNLL-2014, JFLEG, BEA\\
\textbf{Description:} in this notebook we will use BERT and T5 to
predict words in a sentence to perform a spell and grammar checker for
Portuguese and English languages. For English, we will use the BERT and
T5 models from transformers library (huggingface) and evaluate the
performance in CoNLL-2014 and JFLEG datasets. For Portuguese, we will
use the transformers/neuralmind BERT version and a custom dataset for
evaluation.
